Artistic Room Divider

This custom laser-cut steel screen was designed for a luxury residence in the hills, featuring intricate floral patterns inspired by traditional African motifs. The 8ft by 12ft divider serves as both a functional room separator and a stunning art piece in the client's grand foyer.

Our team spent over 200 hours designing and perfecting the pattern to ensure structural integrity while maintaining the delicate appearance. The final product features a powder-coated finish in matte black with hand-applied gold leaf accents that catch the light beautifully.

Project Specifications

Material: 3mm Cold Rolled Steel
Finish: Matte Black Powder Coat + Gold Leaf
Dimensions: 8ft (H) x 12ft (W)
Installation: Floor-mounted with concealed fasteners
Completion: March 2023

Office Building Cladding

This anodized aluminum facade system was engineered for the new corporate headquarters of a Fortune 500 company. The integrated sunshades provide 30% reduction in solar heat gain while maintaining 80% natural light penetration, significantly reducing the building's energy consumption.

Material: 5mm Anodized Aluminum
Finish: Champagne Gold Anodized
Area: 15,000 sq.ft
Energy Savings: 30% reduction in cooling costs

Acoustic Partition Wall

Designed for a cutting-edge tech company's open-plan office, this perforated steel partition system provides both visual separation and acoustic dampening. The geometric pattern was algorithmically designed to optimize sound absorption while creating visual interest.

The system consists of 42 individual panels that can be reconfigured as needed. Each panel features a 2mm perforated steel face with a sound-absorbing fiberglass backing, reducing ambient noise by 60% in the workspace.

Project Specifications

Material: 2mm Perforated Steel + Fiberglass
Finish: Brushed Stainless Steel
Noise Reduction: 60% ambient noise reduction
Modularity: 42 reconfigurable panels
Completion: August 2022

Floating Stair System

This minimalist stainless steel railing system creates the illusion of floating glass panels in a luxury penthouse. The engineered support structure is completely concealed within the glass channels, achieving an ultra-clean aesthetic without compromising safety.

Our team developed custom stainless steel fittings that accommodate thermal expansion of the glass while maintaining perfect alignment. The system meets all international safety standards while appearing nearly invisible.

Project Specifications

Material: 316L Stainless Steel + 12mm Tempered Glass
Finish: Mirror Polished
Length: 28 linear feet
Load Capacity: 400lbs per linear foot
Completion: May 2023

Ventilated Rain Screen

This architectural perforated aluminum facade serves as a ventilated rain screen for a mixed-use development. The system provides weather protection, solar shading, and natural ventilation while creating a dynamic facade that changes appearance throughout the day.

The 25,000 sq.ft installation features custom perforation patterns that vary in density to optimize airflow and shading based on solar orientation. Our team developed a unique clip system that allows for thermal movement while maintaining perfect panel alignment.

Project Specifications

Material: 4mm Perforated Aluminum
Finish: PVDF Fluoropolymer Coating
Area: 25,000 sq.ft
Ventilation: 35% improved airflow vs standard systems
Completion: January 2023

Heritage Balcony Restoration

This historical restoration project involved recreating 19th century wrought iron railings for a heritage-listed building. Our craftsmen used traditional blacksmithing techniques to replicate the original designs while meeting modern safety standards.

We conducted extensive research to match the original patterns and developed a custom patina process to blend the new work with the existing ironwork. The project included 32 balcony sections totaling 420 linear feet of railing.

Project Specifications

Material: Hand-Forged Wrought Iron
Finish: Custom Patina + Protective Wax
Length: 420 linear feet
Techniques: Traditional Blacksmithing
Completion: September 2022
